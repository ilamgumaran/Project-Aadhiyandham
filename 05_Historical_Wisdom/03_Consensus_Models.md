# Models of Consensus Governance

Three proven models for non-rigid, collective decision-making.

## 1. The Haudenosaunee Confederacy
*   **Bicameral:** Consensus must be reached between "Elder" and "Younger" groups.
*   **Veto Power:** "Clan Mothers" hold the power to recall leaders who act against the common will.

## 2. The Zapatista Communities
*   **Mandar Obedeciendo:** "To Lead by Obeying." Leaders are executors, not deciders.
*   **Rotation:** Frequent representative rotation to prevent power consolidation.

## 3. Auroville Selection
*   **Participatory:** Use of deep deliberation to reach a shared "common will" rather than competitive voting.
