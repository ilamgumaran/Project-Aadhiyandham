# The Recycling Cliff

Planning for the exhaustion of salvaged materials.

## Corrosion Timelines
*   **Bare Carbon Steel:** 5–15 years in humid environments before losing structural integrity.
*   **Galvanized Steel:** 35–75 years.
*   **Copper:** 100+ years (highly stable, prioritized for reuse).

## The Transition
The settlement must establish primary smelting (Module 03) within 25 years. Failure to do so will result in a regression to pre-iron technology as the "Old World" mineral battery depletes.
