# The Temperate Antibiotic Kit

Five foundational plants for infection control.

## Botanical Arsenal
1.  **Garlic (*Allium sativum*):** Active: **Allicin**. Raw/crushed for respiratory and digestive infections.
2.  **Thyme (*Thymus vulgaris*):** Active: **Thymol**. Expectorant and skin disinfectant.
3.  **Echinacea:** Immunostimulant for early-stage viral/bacterial threats.
4.  **Sage (*Salvia officinalis*):** Antiseptic for mouth and throat ulcers.
5.  **Plantain (*Plantago lanceolata*):** Active: **Aucubin**. Field treatment for stings, bites, and minor wounds.
