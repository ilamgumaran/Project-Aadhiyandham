# The Brain's Biochemical Signals

Engineering the environment to support the endocrine system.

## Circadian Alignment
*   **Morning Reset:** 10–30 mins of direct outdoor sunlight (2,500 lux minimum) to trigger serotonin.
*   **Adenosine Build-up:** Daily physical labor increases sleep pressure.
*   **Melatonin Release:** Suppression of blue light after sunset to ensure core temperature drop.

## Recovery Cycles
*   **Glymphatic Wash:** Deep sleep triggers the brain’s waste clearance system.
*   **Cortisol Spike:** Early morning light triggers wakefulness and glucose regulation.
