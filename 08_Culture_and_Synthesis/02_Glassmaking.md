# Advanced Synthesis: Glassmaking

Creating optical-grade glass from foundational materials.

## The Pot Furnace
*   **Shell:** 55-gallon drum.
*   **Insulation:** 6 inches of refractory daub (clay/sand/straw).
*   **Crucible (The Pot):** High-alumina clay mixed with grog.

## The Batch Recipe
*   **3 Parts Silica:** Scavenged white sand or crushed clear glass.
*   **1 Part Flux:** Potash leached from hardwood ashes (oak/willow).
*   **0.5 Parts Stabilizer:** Crushed limestone or eggshells.

## Process
*   Maintain forced air (Double-Lung Bellows) to reach 1300°C.
*   Use an **Annealing Lehr** (cooling oven) to prevent shattering during the 24-hour cooling cycle.
