# Precision Metallurgy: Crucible Steel (Wootz)

Refining iron into high-carbon tool steel.

## The Co-Fusion Process
1.  **Charge:** Combine low-carbon wrought iron and high-carbon cast iron (or organic carbon like bamboo) in a sealed clay crucible.
2.  **Melt:** Heat in a high-temp forge until liquid.
3.  **Segregation:** Allow the melt to cool very slowly over several days inside the furnace.
4.  **Result:** Carbon segregates into cementite bands, creating the "watered" pattern and a micro-saw edge.

## Applications
*   Surgical instruments.
*   Fine cutting tools (chisels, planes).
*   High-stress mechanical components (gears, bearings).
