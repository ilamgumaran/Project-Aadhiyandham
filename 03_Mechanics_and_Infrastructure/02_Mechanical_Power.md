# Mechanical Power and the Line-Shaft

Distributing energy without electricity.

## The Line-Shaft System
*   **Main Shaft:** 1.5–2 inch cold-rolled steel.
*   **Pulley Ratios:** Managed by $N_1 \times D_1 = N_2 \times D_2$.
*   **Safety (Fast/Loose):** Use dual pulleys to engage/disengage machines without stopping the main drive.
*   **Maintenance:** Use natural animal-fat or pine-resin based lubricants.
