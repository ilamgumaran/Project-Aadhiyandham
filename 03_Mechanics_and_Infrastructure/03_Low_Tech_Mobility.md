# Low-Tech Mobility

Transportation systems designed for long-term durability.

## Hybrid Bamboo/Steel Cargo Trike
*   **Chassis:** Twin-boom structural bamboo.
*   **Precision Interfaces:** Steel head tubes, bottom brackets, and dropouts (salvaged).
*   **Joining Method:** Fiber-reinforced epoxy or natural resin "lugs."
*   **Capacity:** Design for 250kg useful payload.
