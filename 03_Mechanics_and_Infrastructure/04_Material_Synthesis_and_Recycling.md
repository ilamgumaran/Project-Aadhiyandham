# Material Synthesis and Recycling

Generating primary materials from the earth and ruins.

## Small-Scale Smelting (The Bloomery)
*   **Chimney:** Clay/sand mix, 1m tall.
*   **Fuel:** Hardwood charcoal.
*   **Product:** Wrought iron bloom.

## Chemical Synthesis
*   **Bio-Binders:** Hide glue, pine resin, starch.
*   **Lye/Potash:** Leached from wood ash for soap and tanning.
*   **Tannins:** Oak bark for leather preservation.

## Systematic Recycling
*   Priority: Copper (wiring/pipes) and high-carbon steel (springs).
