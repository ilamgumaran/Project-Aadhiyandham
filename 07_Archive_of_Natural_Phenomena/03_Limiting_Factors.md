# Liebig's Law of the Minimum

The principle of limiting factors in systems.

## The Law
Growth or success is controlled not by the total amount of resources available, but by the **scarcest resource**.

## The Barrel Analogy
Imagine a barrel with staves of different heights. The water (output) can only rise to the level of the shortest stave.

## System Diagnostics
1.  Identify the "shortest stave" (e.g., lack of potassium in soil, or lack of charcoal in the forge).
2.  Prioritize correcting that factor first. 
3.  Adding more of a non-limiting factor will provide zero return on labor.
