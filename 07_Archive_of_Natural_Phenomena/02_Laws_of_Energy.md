# The Laws of Energy

Foundational principles for mechanical and thermal engineering.

## Thermodynamics Summary
*   **First Law (Conservation):** "You can't get something for nothing." Energy cannot be created, only transformed. Energy in = Energy out + Work done.
*   **Second Law (Entropy):** "You can't even break even." No machine is 100% efficient. Useful energy is always lost as waste heat. The universe naturally tends toward disorder.

## Implications for Settlers
*   Prioritize passive systems (solar heat, gravity water).
*   Minimize the number of energy conversions in any machine.
