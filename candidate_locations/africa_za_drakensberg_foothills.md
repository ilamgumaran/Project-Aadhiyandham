# Candidate Location Evaluation: Drakensberg Foothills (Africa)

**Alignment:** Outcome 1: Locating and Connecting Optimal Refugia

## 1. Location Overview
*   **Name:** Drakensberg Foothills (Lower Benches / Foothills)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 29.3833° S, 29.4500° E
*   **Base Elevation:** ~300m - 1,000m - *Note: High-altitude transition.*
*   **Primary Biome:** Montane Grassland / Podocarp Forest

## 2. Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes.
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by the Great Escarpment? Yes. Buffered by the massive basalt peaks of the Drakensberg (3,400m).
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. The **"Water Tower of South Africa"**; source of the Tugela and Orange rivers.
*   **Notes:** Extreme topographical protection and infinite water security.

## 3. Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** High. Consistent inland sunshine; leading region for decentralized solar farms.
*   **Soil Type:** Basalt-derived and mineral-rich. High organic carbon content; requires management of native acidity.
*   **Biomass Availability:** High. Proximity to mountain forests and vast grasslands providing timber and fiber.
*   **Agricultural Score (8.5/10):** Elite for livestock, cool-temperate grains, and tubers. The high-altitude water and solar yield allow for high-quality production.

## 4. Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Foothills are navigable by foot and horse; steep escarpments provide natural boundaries.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Crisp, clear, and high-energy. The majestic mountain backdrop provides a profound sense of security and "geological scale."
*   **Health Score (9/10):** Elite mineral water and air quality. Historically a region of health resorts and pastoral wisdom.

## 5. Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Exceptional access to basalt, sandstone, and clays. Historical center of rock-cut dwellings and pastoral management.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** Naturally defensible via rugged mountain passes. Strategically located between the interior and the coast.

## 6. Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Hydrological Refugia)**
*   **Justification:** The Drakensberg foothills offer the highest level of water security in Southern Africa. Its combination of mountain protection, basalt fertility, and high solar yield makes it a primary refugia.
