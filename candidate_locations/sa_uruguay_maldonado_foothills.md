# Candidate Location Evaluation: Maldonado Foothills, Uruguay (South America)

## Location Overview
*   **Name:** Maldonado Foothills (Sierra de las Ánimas)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Maldonado Department, Uruguay
*   **Base Elevation:** ~100m - 300m
*   **Primary Biome:** Pampa / Hillside Forest (Monte Serrano)

## Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes (the Sierra rises from the coastal plain).
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by a major mountain range? No (it is the range). It provides its own internal protection via deep ravines (quebradas).
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. The Sierra acts as a rain-catchment, feeding numerous permanent streams and springs.
*   **Notes:** One of the most geologically stable regions on Earth (Precambrian rock).

## Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** High. Stable maritime-influenced sunshine with minimal cloud persistence.
*   **Soil Type:** Stony, well-drained soils over volcanic granite and gneiss. Rich in minerals but requires biochar/Terra Preta to build depth for intensive crops.
*   **Biomass Availability:** Moderate. "Monte Serrano" forests provide hard woods; vast grasslands for grazing and fiber.
*   **Agricultural Score (7.5/10):** Elite for livestock and natural pastures. The "quebradas" provide sheltered microclimates for citrus, vines, and medicinal herbs.

## Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Rolling hills are navigable by foot and horse.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Exceptionally stable and mild. The Atlantic influence prevents extreme heat, while the hills buffer the winter winds. Very conducive to the "centered null."
*   **Health Score (9.5/10):** Some of the purest mineral water in South America. High biosecurity due to isolated "pocket" geography.

## Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Exceptional access to granite, gneiss, and quartz. Some of the oldest rock on the planet, providing a "deep time" psychological anchor.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** Naturally defensible via high ridges. Proximity to the Rio de la Plata ruins for scavenging while remaining safely in the "clean" foothills.

## Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Stability Candidate)**
*   **Justification:** For a society prioritizing long-term geological and climatic stability, Maldonado is elite. It offers a peaceful, maritime-buffered environment with the purest water and most ancient foundations available.
