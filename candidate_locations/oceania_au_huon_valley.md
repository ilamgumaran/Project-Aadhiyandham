# Candidate Location Evaluation: Huon Valley, Tasmania (Oceania)

## Location Overview
*   **Name:** Huon Valley
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Southern Tasmania, Australia
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 43.12° S, 147.16° E
*   **Base Elevation:** ~100m - 200m (Benches and Valleys)
*   **Primary Biome:** Cool Temperate Rainforest / Eucalyptus Forest

## Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes (benches above the flood zone).
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by a major mountain range? Yes. Surrounded by the Wellington Range and Southwest Wilderness mountains.
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Extremely high rainfall and reliable river systems.
*   **Notes:** This is a "Climate Lifeboat." It is one of the most temperate and stable environments in the Southern Hemisphere.

## Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** Moderate. High cloud cover, but long summer days. Architecture must maximize "Light on Two Sides."
*   **Soil Type:** Sandy clay loams over heavy clays. Requires lime (Module 01) to manage native acidity.
*   **Biomass Availability:** Extreme. Home to the world's tallest flowering plants (Eucalyptus regnans) and the resilient Huon Pine.
*   **Agricultural Score (8/10):** The "Apple Isle." Elite for cool-temperate fruits, brassicas, and berries. High biomass supports a massive biochar/Terra Preta operation.

## Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** The lower river valley is flat; upper benches require animal power.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Cool, moist, and restorative. The air quality is among the cleanest in the world. Perfect for "Sensory Architecture."
*   **Health Score (9/10):** Exceptionally clean air and water. Naturally cooler temperatures reduce heat stress and tropical disease risks.

## Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Abundant stone and high-quality timber. Some mineral isolation; requires established trade for specialized metals.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** "The Island of the Island." Highly isolated and easily defensible. A natural sanctuary.

## Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (The Global Lifeboat Candidate)**
*   **Justification:** Huon Valley is the ultimate "safety" pick. Its extreme water security and cool-temperate stability make it a primary refuge against global heating.
