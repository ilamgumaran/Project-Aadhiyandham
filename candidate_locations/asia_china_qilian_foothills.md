# Candidate Location Evaluation: Qilian Mountains Foothills (Asia)

## Location Overview
*   **Name:** Qilian Foothills (Hexi Corridor)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Gansu Province, China
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 38.7000° N, 100.7833° E
*   **Base Elevation:** ~300m (Lowest oases) - *Note: Average elevation is higher.*
*   **Primary Biome:** Alpine Meadow / Oasis / Desert transition

## Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes.
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by a major mountain range? Yes. The Qilian Mountains provide a massive hydrological and wind buffer.
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Fed by over 3,000 glaciers and mountain springs.
*   **Notes:** A "Mountain-Oasis" system where the range acts as a water tower for the arid plains below.

## Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** Extreme. One of the highest solar yields in Asia; over 3,000 hours of sunshine per year.
*   **Soil Type:** Grayish-brown carbonaceous and gypseous soils. Fertile when managed with organic matter; naturally supports resilient desert-margin flora.
*   **Biomass Availability:** Moderate. Requires active silviculture; high-altitude meadows provide grazing.
*   **Agricultural Score (8/10):** Elite for wheat, barley, and drought-resistant tubers. "PV + Pastoralism" models prove its high-energy potential.

## Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Excellent. The Hexi Corridor is a broad, flat highway.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Dry and clear. The intense high-altitude light supports an alert, curious mental state.
*   **Health Score (8.5/10):** Extremely low humidity reduces infectious disease. Pure glacial meltwater.

## Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Abundant stone, gypsum, and clays. Proximity to ancient Silk Road knowledge hubs.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** Naturally defensible via mountain passes. A historical corridor for knowledge exchange.

## Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Arid-Refuge Candidate)**
*   **Justification:** For a society focusing on solar synthesis and glacial water security, the Qilian foothills are elite. It provides a unique model for "thriving in the rain shadow."
