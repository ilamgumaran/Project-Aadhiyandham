# Candidate Location Evaluation: Lord Howe Island Benches (Oceania)

**Alignment:** Outcome 1: Locating and Connecting Optimal Refugia

## 1. Location Overview
*   **Name:** Lord Howe Island (High-Elevation Benches)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Tasman Sea, Australia (600km offshore)
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 31.55° S, 159.08° E
*   **Base Elevation:** ~100m (Lower slopes) to 600m (Benches)
*   **Primary Biome:** Cloud Forest / Subtropical Rainforest

## 2. Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes.
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by major peaks? Yes. Protected by Mt. Gower and Mt. Lidgbird.
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Cloud-trapping peaks ensure constant moisture and springs.
*   **Notes:** A unique "Sky Island" refugium with extreme geographic isolation.

## 3. Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** Moderate to High. Clear maritime skies, though cloud forests are often misty.
*   **Soil Type:** Volcanic (Basalt-derived). Mineral-rich and highly fertile, though thin on the higher benches.
*   **Biomass Availability:** Moderate. Unique endemic forests (Big Mountain Palm); requires careful conservation.
*   **Agricultural Score (7/10):** Specialized. Elite for high-value endemic crops and maritime resources. Limited flat land for large-scale grains.

## 4. Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Difficult. Steep volcanic terrain requires well-maintained footpaths and manual transport.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Stable and moist. The "sky forest" aesthetic is profoundly peaceful and conducive to the "centered null."
*   **Health Score (9.5/10):** Cleanest air/water in the world. Naturally isolated from global pathogens.

## 5. Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Abundant basalt and marine resources.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** The ultimate isolation. Virtually unreachable without intentional navigation. Defensible via "Natural Moat" (the Tasman Sea).

## 6. Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Specialized Isolated Refuge)**
*   **Justification:** Lord Howe Island is the premier "micro-refuge." For a small, high-knowledge society, its combination of biosecurity and volcanic stability is unmatched globally.
