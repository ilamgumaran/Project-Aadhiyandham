# Candidate Location Evaluation: Sarakata River Basin, Vanuatu

## Location Overview
*   **Name:** Sarakata River Basin (Foothills)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Espiritu Santo Island, Vanuatu
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 15.3900° S, 166.8500° E
*   **Base Elevation:** ~100m - 300m (Marine terraces and foothills)
*   **Primary Biome:** Tropical Rainforest

## Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes.
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by complex volcanic terrain? Yes. The deep basin and forested hills provide thermal buffering.
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Fed by deep limestone aquifers and the Sarakata river system.
*   **Notes:** A critical refugium during El Niño droughts; high water security.

## Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** High. Consistent tropical solar gain.
*   **Soil Type:** Rich volcanic and limestone-derived soils. Extremely fertile.
*   **Biomass Availability:** Extreme. Dense rainforest and high growth rates.
*   **Agricultural Score (9/10):** Exceptional for tropical staples and specialized "ridge-to-reef" horticulture.

## Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Foothills and terraces are navigable; river corridors provide transport.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Warm and stable. The high-altitude tropical climate is restorative.
*   **Health Score (8/10):** Exceptional air and water quality.

## Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Abundant volcanic rock and limestone for building. Proximity to renewable hydropower potential.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** Isolated island location; defensible via terrain and forest.

## Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Pacific Refugia)**
*   **Justification:** Combines volcanic fertility with unmatched water security and extreme biomass growth rates.
