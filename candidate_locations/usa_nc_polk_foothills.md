# Candidate Location Evaluation: Polk County Foothills, NC (USA)

## Location Overview
*   **Name:** Polk County Foothills (The Thermal Belt)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Western North Carolina, USA (Tryon / Columbus / Saluda)
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 35.2086° N, 82.2508° W
*   **Base Elevation:** ~250m - 350m (800 - 1,100 ft)
*   **Primary Biome:** Temperate Deciduous Forest

## Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes (~300m).
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by a major mountain range? Yes. Nestled against the Blue Ridge Escarpment.
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Fed by the Green and Broad river headwaters and mountain springs.
*   **Notes:** Home to the world-renowned **Thermal Belt** phenomenon, where a stable layer of warm air prevents frost, extending the growing season by weeks.

## Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** High. The Blue Ridge blocks many Atlantic cloud systems; clear skies are common.
*   **Soil Type:** Fertile, dark brown clay loams (**Skyuka** series). Naturally acidic; requires lime (Module 01) but excellent for organic matter retention.
*   **Biomass Availability:** Extremely High. Dense hardwood forests provide abundant timber, charcoal, and nut resources.
*   **Agricultural Score (9/10):** Elite for stone fruits, grapes, and berries. The thermal belt makes it a premiere northern-latitude refuge for tender crops.

## Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Rolling to flat valley floors are easily navigated by all non-electric transport.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Stable and mild. The "Tryon Blue" light and clean mountain air support an alert and restorative mental state.
*   **Health Score (9/10):** High mineral water security and low climatic stress due to the thermal inversion.

## Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Abundant stone, sand, and river gravels. Historically part of the Appalachian iron-making region.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** Naturally defensible via the "Gaps" in the escarpment. Strategically located between the ruins of Charlotte and Asheville for long-term salvage.

## Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Thermal Candidate)**
*   **Justification:** Polk County is a meteorological miracle. Its combination of mountain protection, thermal belt stability, and fertile soil makes it one of the most resilient sites in the Eastern US.
