# Candidate Location Evaluation: Bac Son Limestone Mountains, Vietnam

## Location Overview
*   **Name:** Bac Son Limestone Mountains (Karst Slopes)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Lang Son Province, Northern Vietnam
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 21.8438° N, 106.2993° E
*   **Base Elevation:** ~150m - 300m
*   **Primary Biome:** Closed Evergreen Tropical Forest on Limestone

## Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes.
*   [x] **Protection:** Shielded by towering limestone peaks? Yes. Deep valleys and cave systems provide extreme thermal buffering.
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Deep karst aquifers and mountain runoff.
*   **Notes:** Historically stable climate; part of a globally significant limestone refugium.

## Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** High. Consistent tropical solar gain, slightly buffered by cloud cover.
*   **Soil Type:** Rich alluvial and limestone-derived forest loams. High fertility.
*   **Biomass Availability:** High. Dense forest on the karst slopes provide diverse timber and medicinal resources.
*   **Agricultural Score (9/10):** Exceptional for rice, tubers, and traditional vegetables.

## Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Valley floors are flat and easily traversed; caves provide immediate shelter.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Stable and tranquil. The dramatic karst landscape supports a sense of enclosure and security.
*   **Health Score (8.5/10):** Exceptional air and water quality.

## Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Infinite limestone for building. Ancient history of "Bac Son Culture" resource management.
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** Naturally defensible via narrow mountain passes.

## Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Karst Refugia)**
*   **Justification:** Combines fertile valley floors with towering mountain walls and reliable underground water. A proven cradle of civilization.
