# Candidate Location Evaluation: Lorentz Lowlands, Papua (Asia)

**Alignment:** Outcome 1: Locating and Connecting Optimal Refugia

## Location Overview
*   **Name:** Lorentz Lowlands (Tidal Swamps / Alluvial Fans)
*   **Coordinates/Region:** Southern Papua, Indonesia (Lorentz National Park)
*   **GPS Coordinates:** 4.7500° S, 137.8333° E
*   **Base Elevation:** ~100m - 300m (Foothills above the tidal zone)
*   **Primary Biome:** Tropical Rainforest / Wetland transition

## Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)
*   [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes (benches above the tidal zone).
*   [x] **Protection:** Buffered by massive forest? Yes. Protected by the dense Papuan canopy and high peaks (Mt. Carstensz).
*   [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Fed by glacial meltwater and high-altitude waterfalls.
*   **Notes:** A "Continuous Transect" refugium; one of the only places on Earth with glaciers in the tropics.

## Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance
*   **Solar Potential:** Moderate to High. Consistent tropical solar yield, buffered by cloud-trapping peaks.
*   **Soil Type:** Alluvial fans and forest loams. Extremely rich in organic matter due to the ancient forest cycle.
*   **Biomass Availability:** Extreme. The most biodiverse and intact forest block in the Asia-Pacific.
*   **Agricultural Score (8.5/10):** Elite for sago, tropical tubers (Taro), and medicinal plants. The "Glacial Water" effect ensures year-round productivity.

## Phase III: The Human System
*   **Navigability:** Difficult. River systems provide the primary transport corridors; dense forest requires animal-powered clearing.
*   **Climate & Mood:** Humid and vibrant. The "soundscape of the lungs of the world" supports deep sensory connection.
*   **Health Score (7/10):** World-class air quality. Tropical environment requires strict management of pathogens and vector control.

## Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis
*   **Geological Foundations:** Abundant stone, river gravels, and clays. Proximity to mountain mineral deposits (e.g., Grasberg zone).
*   **Isolation vs. Network:** The ultimate isolation. Reachable only by river or narrow mountain paths. A "Natural Fortress of Green."

## Final Verdict
*   **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Tropical Biodiversity Candidate)**
*   **Justification:** Lorentz is the ultimate "safety" pick for Asia. Its combination of glacial water security and megadiversity makes it a primary lifeboat for the tropical world.
