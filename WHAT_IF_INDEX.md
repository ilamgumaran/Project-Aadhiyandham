# The What-If Index (என்ன ஆனால்?)

**A searchable guide for the moment the plan stops working.**
Companions: [pressure_test_report.md](pressure_test_report.md) & [settling_pressure_test_report.md](settling_pressure_test_report.md) (the audits these scenarios came from) · [CONSEQUENCES_OF_DEVIATION.md](CONSEQUENCES_OF_DEVIATION.md) (what it costs to ignore a critical instruction, and which numbers are exact-or-you-die).

> **How to use this index.** Find your question (search the page for a keyword: *water, injured, occupied, checkpoint, bored, winter…*), read the first moves, then go to the deep module. And hold one thing in mind while you do: **this index does not think for you — it gets you to a starting point.** Every entry is a rehearsed hypothesis, not a script; your ground, your people, and your own tested judgment outrank this page the moment they disagree with it. That is not a disclaimer, it is the method of the whole manual: read → practice → observe → revise. The traveler who has rehearsed ten of these scenarios will handle the eleventh — the one this index missed — better than any book could specify. That traveler is the goal, not this list.

## A. Before Departure

| What if… | First moves | Go deeper |
|:---|:---|:---|
| …society is functioning normally — is this whole plan pointless? | No — it works *best* now: build legally, gradually, with hardware stores and doctors still available. | …I don't have a group — where do I even start? | Here. The founding group is the project's long-lead item and the #1 cause of failure is internal, not external. | …I've only read the manual — can I actually do any of this? | Reading is not skill. ## D. The First Year

| What if… | First moves | Go deeper |
|:---|:---|:---|
| …we're short of salt and the harvest needs preserving? | Salt is preservation infrastructure, not seasoning — without it the harvest rots. Source it (solar salt-pan on a coast, boil brine springs, refine rock salt) or, inland, secure it as a planned trade good. Never under-salt to stretch supply — preserve *less*, properly. | [Salt Production & Sourcing](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/04_Food_and_Soil_Sovereignty/12_Salt_Production_and_Sourcing.md) |
| …we need reliable protein fast, before a herd can exist? | The fast cycles: passive fish traps and a stocked pond (protein while you sleep); poultry (near-daily eggs + pest control) and rabbits (efficient meat — but always with a fat source, or rabbit starvation). Lock stock up every night against predators. | [Fishing & Aquaculture](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/06_Ecological_Harmony/08_Fishing_and_Aquaculture.md), [Small Livestock](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/06_Ecological_Harmony/09_Small_Livestock_Poultry_and_Rabbits.md) |
| …the crops set poorly / we want more yield and a sweetener? | Keep bees — pollination is a yield multiplier across the whole food system, and the hive also gives honey (food + wound medicine) and wax (light, sealing). Take only surplus honey; leave the colony its winter stores; no honey to infants. | [Beekeeping](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/06_Ecological_Harmony/10_Beekeeping.md) |
| …the first crop underperforms badly? | It usually does (30–60% of plan is the historical norm) — which is exactly why the reserve must be planned at that discount, not full yield. Deploy seed diversity, wild staples, and the carried margin; adjust the second planting from the first's real data. | [First-Year Food Security & Arrival Timing §4](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/04_Food_and_Soil_Sovereignty/10_First_Year_Food_Security_and_Arrival_Timing.md), [Emergency Nutrition](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/04_Food_and_Soil_Sovereignty/06_Emergency_Nutrition.md) |
| …how do we stop disease before it starts (not just react)? | The humblest medicine is the strongest: hand-washing with soap, regular laundry and sun-drying (lice/fleas live in cloth and carry typhus/plague), rodent control, and draining standing water. This daily discipline prevents the outbreaks the epidemic protocol can only react to. | [Hygiene, Laundry & Parasite Control](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/05_Health_and_the_Body/12_Hygiene_Laundry_and_Parasite_Control.md) |
| …how do we see and work after dark without wrecking sleep? | Make light from tallow, oil, and beeswax (rushlights, oil lamps, candles) — and keep it dim, warm, and low, which flame-light naturally is, so it doubles as circadian protection. Put every flame out before sleep. | [Lighting: Lamps & Candles](Outcome_5_Decadal_Resilience/09_The_Mechanics/10_Lighting_Lamps_and_Candles.md) |
| …how do we recover the nutrients in urine safely? | Divert it (a simple seat/urinal): it's nearly sterile and carries most of the N-P-K. Dilute ~1:10 for growing crops (undiluted burns them), use it neat in compost and to charge biochar, and keep it away from water (its nitrogen is the blue-baby nitrate if it reaches a well). | [Urine Diversion & Nutrient Recovery](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/03_Water_and_Sanitation/08_Urine_Diversion_and_Nutrient_Recovery.md) |
| …people start getting stomach illness? | Assume your own water/sanitation loop first — it is almost always the latrine, the hands, or the "clean" source. Re-verify the chain end to end; boil everything until found. | [Immediate Sanitation](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/03_Water_and_Sanitation/03_Immediate_Sanitation.md), [Thermophilic Sanitation Math](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/03_Water_and_Sanitation/06_Thermophilic_Sanitation_Math.md) |
| …the water is clear and tastes fine but a baby goes blue, or people sicken slowly over years? | Boiling and sand filters do **not** remove chemical poisons — nitrate (blue-baby), arsenic, metals — and boiling arsenic *concentrates* it. Switch infants/pregnancy to rainwater or a known-clean upstream source now; assess the source by where it's been and the local geology. | [Water Contaminant Testing](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/03_Water_and_Sanitation/07_Water_Contaminant_Testing_and_Remediation.md), [Consequences §4](CONSEQUENCES_OF_DEVIATION.md) |
| …stored grain/preserves look or smell slightly off — can we still eat them? | For four things, **no, and cooking won't fix it**: ergot (dark curved grains), mold/aflatoxin, any doubtful anaerobic preserve (botulism — never taste to check), and warm-held cooked greens. Discard the batch; the hunger of waste is survivable, these are not. | [Food Toxins & Storage Safety](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/04_Food_and_Soil_Sovereignty/09_Food_Toxins_and_Storage_Safety.md), [Consequences §6](CONSEQUENCES_OF_DEVIATION.md) |
| …by late winter people are tired, irritable, gums bleeding, a child not thriving? | Read it as a **deficiency alarm**, not morale: scurvy hits in 6–12 weeks of no vitamin C; the staple traps (pellagra from un-nixtamalized maize, beriberi from over-milled grain) punish monotony. Diversify now — needle tea, kraut, sprouts, whole grain, animal fat. | [Deficiency Diseases & Heat Illness](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/05_Health_and_the_Body/10_Deficiency_Diseases_and_Heat_Illness.md) |
| …a worker gets dizzy and confused in the heat? | The danger line is **sweating stops / confusion starts** = heat stroke, fatal in hours. Pull them, cool hard and immediately (water, wet-and-fan, cool packs to neck/armpits/groin). Heat exhaustion (still sweating) recovers with shade, water, rest. | [Deficiency Diseases & Heat Illness §4](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/05_Health_and_the_Body/10_Deficiency_Diseases_and_Heat_Illness.md) |
| …I'm not sure which instructions I must follow exactly vs. can adapt? | Learn the three tiers: **Law** (exact — pH 4.6, 55°C×15 days, 30 m setback, CO vent — measure, never round), **Craft** (a range you calibrate by a test), **Preference** (adapt freely). Rounding a Law kills; over-obeying a Craft wastes effort. | [CONSEQUENCES_OF_DEVIATION.md](CONSEQUENCES_OF_DEVIATION.md), [Settling Pressure Test](settling_pressure_test_report.md) |
| …serious injury or a difficult birth happens here, without a hospital? | The trauma and midwifery protocols are the best zero-tech medicine available — and the manual owes you honesty: some events they cannot save. Prevention culture (sanitation, dental, no risky solo work) is where the real mortality reduction lives. | [Advanced Trauma Care](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/05_Health_and_the_Body/04_Advanced_Trauma_Care.md), [Midwifery and Childbirth](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/05_Health_and_the_Body/07_Midwifery_and_Childbirth.md), [Pressure Test §5.5](pressure_test_report.md) |
| …predators or crop raiders find the fields? | Layered coexistence, not eradication: exclusion for the garden core, guardians and deterrence for the ring, tolerance at the edge. | [Crop Raider Defense](Outcome_3_Perimeter_Defense/07b_Animal_Coexistence_Protocols/03_Crop_Raider_Defense.md), [Livestock Guardian Systems](Outcome_3_Perimeter_Defense/07b_Animal_Coexistence_Protocols/04_Livestock_Guardian_Systems.md) |
| …wildfire threatens the site? | The defensible space you cut in month one is the plan; ignition discipline and the fire-break ring are maintenance, not crisis response. | [Wildfire Defensible Space](Outcome_3_Perimeter_Defense/07_Passive_Defense/03_Wildfire_Defensible_Space.md) |
| …the house catches fire from its own hearth? | Prevented by design: stone hearth surround, wide clearance, clean flue, **two exits drilled in the dark**, water+sand at every flame. Smother a fat fire (never water). Keep seed and reserve stored away from any flame so one fire isn't a starvation event. | [Fire Safety and the Hearth](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/06_Ecological_Harmony/07_Fire_Safety_and_the_Hearth.md) |
| …a crop disease or pest outbreak hits the fields? | Diversity is the defense (no crop/variety >30%); walk the fields and **rogue at first sight** (burn/bury infected material, never compost it); take seed only from healthy plants. If it's winning, secure the clean seed line first, then sacrifice the crop — the harvest is one year, a lost variety is forever. | [Crop Disease & Pest Response](Outcome_2_Biological_Sovereignty/04_Food_and_Soil_Sovereignty/11_Crop_Disease_and_Pest_Response.md) |
| …outsiders discover the settlement? | First contact is a protocol, not an improvisation: observation, graduated disclosure, bounded generosity — the settlement-side versions of the road doctrine. | [First Contact Protocols](Outcome_6_Flourishing_Civilization/14a_Inter_Settlement_Diplomacy/02_First_Contact_Protocols.md), [De-escalation](Outcome_3_Perimeter_Defense/07a_Active_Threat_Response/03_De_escalation_and_Non_Lethal_Deterrence.md) |
| …there's an epidemic — in the region, or already inside? | The whole networking doctrine inverts: **quarantine every arrival** (2 weeks default), barrier-nurse the sick with soap and dedicated/immune carers, bury the dead safely, and climb the sealing ladder (watch → restrict → seal) — decided in calm, food reserve making it survivable. | …a group demands entry or tribute? | Lockdown posture buys time; de-escalation and visible not-worth-it-ness do the work. Passive defense buys cost and delay, never immunity — remoteness and alliances are the real wall. | [Emergency Lockdown](Outcome_3_Perimeter_Defense/07a_Active_Threat_Response/04_Emergency_Lockdown_Procedures.md), [Pressure Test §6.2](pressure_test_report.md) |

Mediation protocol, early, with the full circle behind it. | [Conflict Resolution](Outcome_4_Psychological_Centeredness/08_The_Mind/04_Conflict_Resolution.md), [Power Vacuum Prevention](Outcome_4_Psychological_Centeredness/08a_Group_Dynamics/02_Power_Vacuum_Prevention.md) |

## The scenario this index missed

There will be one. When you meet it: stop, breathe once the long way out, and run the method every module here runs — *what do I observe (not assume)? what does it threaten first (water > warmth > safety > food)? what's the cheapest reversible move?* Make that move, observe again, and write down what happened. Then — when there is time — add the scenario to this index for the next traveler. That final step is not optional housekeeping; it is how a manual stays alive, and how you repay the strangers who wrote the entries that got you this far.
