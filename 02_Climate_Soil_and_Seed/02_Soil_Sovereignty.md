# Soil Sovereignty: The Terra Preta Method

## 1. Why This Is Important
Standard agriculture extracts nutrients from the soil, eventually leading to collapse. **Terra Preta** (Amazonian Dark Earth) is a regenerative technology. By adding **Biochar** (highly porous charcoal), we create a permanent "biological reef" in the soil. This reef holds water, provides a home for beneficial microbes, and prevents nutrients from washing away. Once established, this soil grows more fertile every year.

## 2. Expected Outcome
A deep, black, nutrient-rich topsoil that requires 50% less water and zero synthetic fertilizers, capable of supporting high-intensity agriculture for centuries.

## 3. How-To: Producing Raw Biochar (The Cone-Pit Method)
The goal is to burn biomass without oxygen (**Pyrolysis**).

### Materials Needed
*   **Dry Biomass:** Sticks, corn stalks, or timber scraps.
*   **The Pit:** A cone-shaped hole in the ground (1m deep, 1.5m wide at the top).
*   **Water:** To quench the fire at the end.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1.  **The Start:** Build a small fire at the very bottom of the cone pit.
2.  **The Cap:** Once the first layer is burning well, add a thin layer of biomass on top. The flame from the bottom will consume the oxygen, preventing the new layer from turning to ash.
3.  **The Layering:** Continue adding layers as soon as you see a grey ash film start to form on the previous layer. Keep the pit "capped" with rising smoke/flame.
4.  **The Quench:** When the pit is full of glowing coals, immediately soak the entire pit with water. 
5.  **Result:** High-quality, "crunchy" biochar that is almost pure carbon.

---

## 4. How-To: The Activation Protocol (Charging)
**CRITICAL:** Never add raw biochar directly to your plants. Its high porosity will "steal" nutrients from the plants to fill its pores. You must "charge" it first.

### Step-by-Step Instructions
1.  **Crush:** Break the biochar into pea-sized pieces.
2.  **Mix (50:50):** Combine **1 bucket of Biochar** with **1 bucket of active Compost or Manure**.
3.  **Mineralize:** Add 1kg of crushed eggshells (Calcium) and 1kg of wood ash (Potassium) per cubic meter.
4.  **Age:** Keep the mixture moist and shaded for **4 weeks**.
5.  **Outcome:** The pores of the biochar are now filled with microbes and nutrients, ready to be released to your crops.

## 5. Application and Maintenance
*   **Initial Dose:** Mix the charged biochar into the top 15cm of your garden beds at a **10% volume ratio**.
*   **Maintenance:** No further biochar is needed. Simply add organic mulch (straw/leaves) to the surface every season to keep the "biological reef" fed.
