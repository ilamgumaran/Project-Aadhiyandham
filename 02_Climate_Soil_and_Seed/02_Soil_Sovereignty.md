# Soil Sovereignty: The Terra Preta Method

Creating carbon-sequestering, high-fertility dark earth.

## The Activation Protocol
1.  **50:50 Mix:** Combine 1 part Raw Biochar with 1 part active Compost or Manure.
2.  **Mineral Load:** Add crushed limestone, bone meal, or eggshells.
3.  **Maturation:** Wait 4 weeks for microbial colonization.

## Application Rate
*   Integrate at **10% by volume** into the topsoil.
*   *Result:* Dramatic increases in water retention, nutrient density, and long-term yield.
