# Livestock and Mechanical Animals

Integrating animals as tools and nutrient engines.

## Work-to-Feed Efficiency
*   **Oxen:** Highest efficiency for heavy labor on low-quality forage. Best for primary land clearing and heavy plowing.
*   **Mules:** Superior endurance and heat tolerance. Best for transport and consistent field work.
*   **Draft Horses:** High speed but low feed efficiency. Reserve for time-critical tasks.

## Nutrient Cycling
*   **Manure:** The primary input for charging Biochar.
*   **Integrated Grazing:** Rotating animals through forest and orchard systems to improve soil health without synthetic inputs.
