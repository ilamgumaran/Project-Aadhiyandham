# The Seed Bank: The 10 Universal Staples

Prioritizing heirloom varieties with maximum caloric and nutritional density.

## The Elite List
1.  **Taro:** (18-38M cal/acre) Maximum tropical/wetland production.
2.  **Potatoes:** (15-25M cal/acre) Vitamin C and high-density starch.
3.  **Cassava:** (12-15M cal/acre) Drought-tolerant starch.
4.  **Sweet Potatoes:** (10-15M cal/acre) High Vitamin A and long-term storage.
5.  **Sunchokes:** (10-12M cal/acre) Minimum maintenance, iron-rich.
6.  **Corn (Flint):** (5-8M cal/acre) Flour production and nutrient density.
7.  **Quinoa:** (3-5M cal/acre) Complete protein source.
8.  **Amaranth:** (3-5M cal/acre) High protein and iron.
9.  **Buckwheat:** Rapid 60-day recovery crop.
10. **Millet:** Extreme drought resistance and multi-year storage life.
