# Shelter and Thermal Grounding

Establishing a stable internal environment is the second priority.

## Construction Methods
*   **Wattle and Daub:** 
    *   *Rationale:* High insulation, fast assembly.
    *   *Target:* Sleeping and recovery quarters.
*   **Rammed Earth:**
    *   *Rationale:* High thermal mass (the "flywheel effect").
    *   *Target:* Communal kitchens and food storage.

## Design Rule
*   Walls must be 18+ inches thick for rammed earth to achieve a 10-12 hour thermal lag.
