# Bio-Security: Water Purification

To support a pioneer population of 50, a **Slow Sand Filter (SSF)** with a Biochar stage is the primary objective.

## Technical Specifications
*   **Capacity:** 2,000 Liters/day (40L per person).
*   **Design:** A vertical tank (1.05m diameter, 2.5m height).

## Layering Protocol (Top to Bottom)
1.  **Water Head (1.0m):** Provides gravity pressure.
2.  **Bio-Layer (Schmutzdecke):** Pathogen consumption zone.
3.  **Fine Sand (0.8m):** 0.15–0.35mm washed masonry sand.
4.  **Biochar/Charcoal (0.2m):** Chemical adsorption.
5.  **Graded Gravel (0.3m):** Structural support.

## Maintenance
*   Maintain 24/7 flow.
*   "Wet" maintenance only; never allow the sand bed to dry out.
