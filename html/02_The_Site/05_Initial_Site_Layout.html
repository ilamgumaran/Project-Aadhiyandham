Initial Site Layout: The 6 Zones of Permaculture

1. Why This Is Important

Without a logical layout, a settlement wastes 40% of its caloric energy on inefficient movement. By organizing the site into Zones, we ensure that high-maintenance elements are close to the center.

The 6 Zones of Permaculture

Z0 is the hearth. Move outward as interaction frequency decreases.

Step-by-Step Zoning

  • Zone 0: The Home. Internal grounding and recovery.
  • Zone 1 (Daily): Kitchen garden, water filter, and nursery.
  • Zone 2 (Every 2 Days): Poultry and small fruit trees.
  • Zone 3 (Weekly): Main crop staples (Potatoes/Corn) and large livestock.
  • Zone 4 (Seasonal): Timber production and wild foraging.
  • Zone 5 (Observation): Unmanaged wilderness.
