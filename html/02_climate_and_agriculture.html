02 Climate, Soil & Seed

1. Soil Sovereignty

The **Terra Preta** method is our standard for building regenerative, high-fertility soil.

How-To: The Terra Preta Recipe

  1. Produce Biochar: Pyrolyze biomass (Module 03) to create raw charcoal.
  2. Charge (Activate): Mix **1 Part Biochar with 1 Part Compost/Manure**.
  3. Infuse: Add crushed eggshells (minerals) and bone meal.
  4. Age: Keep moist for 4 weeks to allow microbial colonization.
  5. Apply: Mix into top 10cm of soil at a **10% volume ratio**.
