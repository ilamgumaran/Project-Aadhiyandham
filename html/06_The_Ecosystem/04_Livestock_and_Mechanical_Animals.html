Livestock and Mechanical Animals: Management

1. Biological Tools

Animals provide mechanical energy for work and biochemical energy (manure) for soil fertility.

Rotational Grazing (The Silvopasture Loop)

  1. Meadow: High grass grazing (3-5 days).
  2. Orchard: Understory clearing and pest control.
  3. Recovery: Move to new zone; allow 30 days for regrowth and manure integration.

The Nutrient Loop

Grass -> Animal -> Manure -> Biochar Charging -> Garden Soil -> High-Yield Crops -> Back to Grass
