1. The Biological Reef

By adding **Biochar**, we create a permanent home for microbes and prevent nutrient leaching.

The Cone-Pit Kiln

How-To: Build a Soil Sieve

Before mixing Terra Preta, refine your sub-soil with this tool:

  • Frame: Square timber frame (60cm x 60cm).
  • Mesh: 1/4 inch (6mm) hardware cloth or wire mesh.
  • Usage: Shovel raw soil onto the mesh and shake over a wheelbarrow to remove rocks.

The Activation Protocol

  1. Crush: Break biochar into pea-sized pieces.
  2. Mix (50:50): Combine 1 part Biochar with 1 part Compost/Manure.
  3. Mineralize: Add crushed eggshells and wood ash.
  4. Age: Keep moist for 4 weeks before use.

Vocabulary of the Foundation

  • Pyrolysis: Breaking down biomass using high heat without oxygen to produce biochar.
  • CEC: Cation Exchange Capacity; the soil's ability to hold onto nutrients.
  • Mycorrhizal Fungi: Beneficial fungi that help roots find water and minerals.
