Candidate Location Evaluation: Ebro Valley Terraces, Spain (Europe)


Location Overview

  • **Name:** Ebro Valley (Pre-Pyrenees Terraces)
  • **Coordinates/Region:** Aragon / Navarre, Spain
  • **GPS Coordinates:** 41.6561° N, 0.8773° W
  • **Base Elevation:** ~200m - 300m
  • **Primary Biome:** Mediterranean Sclerophyllous Forest / Steppe

    • Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)

  • [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes (~200m).
  • [x] **Protection:** Shielded by a major mountain range? Yes. Protected by the Pyrenees to the north and the Iberian System to the south.
  • [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. The Ebro River and its Pyrenean tributaries (Segre, Cinca) provide massive snowmelt security.
  • **Notes:** A "Little Continent" within Spain, characterized by intense mountain protection and high solar gain.

    • Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance

  • **Solar Potential:** Exceptional. One of the highest solar yields in Europe.
  • **Soil Type:** Limestone and gypsum-rich alluvial soils. High mineral content; requires management of salinization via traditional terracing (Module 01).
  • **Biomass Availability:** Moderate. Holm oak and pine; requires careful silviculture to manage fuel stocks.
  • **Agricultural Score (9/10):** Elite for grains, olives, almonds, and vines. The combination of Pyrenean water and Spanish sun is powerful.

    • Phase III: The Human System

  • **Navigability:** Excellent. The valley floor and terraces are historical transport corridors.
  • **Climate & Mood:** Dry, luminous, and clear. The "Cierzo" (down-valley wind) clears the air and supports a sharp, objective mental state.
  • **Health Score (8.5/10):** Low humidity and high solar intensity ensure high Vitamin D and low incidence of infectious disease.

    • Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis

  • **Geological Foundations:** Infinite limestone and gypsum (for mortar and plaster). Ancient history of irrigation and stone architecture.
  • **Isolation vs. Network:** Strategically defensible via mountain passes. A historical "refugia" during multiple European upheavals.

    • Final Verdict

  • **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Mediterranean Candidate)**
  • **Justification:** The Ebro Valley offers the highest solar-to-water balance in Europe. Its combination of Pyrenean "Water Tower" security and intense Mediterranean sun makes it a premier site for a synthesis-based society.
