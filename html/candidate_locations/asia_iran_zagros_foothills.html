Candidate Location Evaluation: Zagros Foothills, Ilam (Asia)


Location Overview

  • **Name:** Zagros Foothills (Ilam Province)
  • **Coordinates/Region:** Western Iran (Bordering Iraq)
  • **Base Elevation:** ~300m (Lower slopes and basins)
  • **Primary Biome:** Zagrosian Oak Forest / Semi-Arid Shrubland

    • Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)

  • [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes (~300m base).
  • [x] **Protection:** Shielded by a major mountain range? Yes. The high Zagros peaks block cold northern air and trap Mediterranean moisture.
  • [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Fed by mountain springs and the Karun/Karkheh watersheds.
  • **Notes:** One of the oldest "cradles of agriculture" on Earth (e.g., Chogha Golan site).

    • Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance

  • **Solar Potential:** Extreme. One of the highest solar yields globally; clear skies for most of the year.
  • **Soil Type:** Calcareous (lime-rich). Excellent for early-stage cereals and legumes. Requires management of organic matter in drier sections.
  • **Biomass Availability:** Moderate. Extensive oak forests provide slow-growing but high-quality structural timber and charcoal.
  • **Agricultural Score (8.5/10):** Elite for heirloom grains (wild wheat/barley), lentils, and pomegranates. Ancient heritage of terrace farming provides a structural template.

    • Phase III: The Human System

  • **Navigability:** Lower basins and valleys are easily traversed by foot and animal power.
  • **Climate & Mood:** Warm and dry. The intense light and ancient landscape provide a deep sense of "ancestral continuity."
  • **Health Score (8/10):** High mineral content in food and water. Low humidity reduces common infectious disease vectors.

    • Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis

  • **Geological Foundations:** Infinite limestone and clays. Proximity to ancient mining regions (copper/silver) in the high Zagros.
  • **Isolation vs. Network:** Naturally defensible via rugged mountain passes. Strategically located between the Mesopotamian ruins and the Iranian plateau.

    • Final Verdict

  • **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Ancient Heritage Candidate)**
  • **Justification:** Ilam is a "proven" location for foundational civilization. Its unique combination of high solar yield, mountain protection, and the genetic birthplace of modern crops makes it an elite site for re-establishing life.
