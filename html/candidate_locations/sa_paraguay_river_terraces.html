Candidate Location Evaluation: Paraguay River Terraces (South America)


Location Overview

  • **Name:** Paraguay River Terraces (Oriental Region)
  • **Coordinates/Region:** Eastern Paraguay (Concepción / San Pedro)
  • **GPS Coordinates:** -23.4380° S, 58.4483° W
  • **Base Elevation:** ~100m - 200m
  • **Primary Biome:** Humid Subtropical Forest / Savanna (Cerrado)

    • Phase I: Placement & Arrival (Pass/Fail Criteria)

  • [x] **Elevation:** 100m+ above sea level? Yes (terraces above the Pantanal).
  • [x] **Protection:** No major range shield. Protection is provided by distance from the ocean and internal forest buffering.
  • [x] **Gravity Water:** Year-round water sources from higher elevation? Yes. Fed by the Paraguay River and eastern highland springs.
  • **Notes:** The terraces offer stability and flood-protection compared to the lower alluvial plains.

    • Phase II: Stabilization & Sustenance

  • **Solar Potential:** Very High. Consistent subtropical sunshine with high direct irradiance.
  • **Soil Type:** **Terra Rossa** (laterite) and fluric alluvial soils. High fertility but vulnerable to erosion; requires no-till management.
  • **Biomass Availability:** High. A mix of humid forest and Cerrado savanna providing diverse timber and fiber.
  • **Agricultural Score (8.5/10):** Elite for grains (corn/soy), cassava, and silvopastoral systems. High growth rates support rapid food sovereignty.

    • Phase III: The Human System

  • **Navigability:** Excellent. The flat-to-rolling river terraces are ideal for non-electric transport.
  • **Climate & Mood:** Warm and humid. The vast, open river landscape provides a sense of "hydrological flow" and connectivity.
  • **Health Score (7/10):** Abundant freshwater. High heat and humidity require strict management of tropical disease vectors.

    • Phase IV & V: Wisdom & Synthesis

  • **Geological Foundations:** Rich in sedimentary clays and iron-rich laterites.
  • **Isolation vs. Network:** The river is a primary trade highway. Deep inland positioning provides defensive depth against maritime disruptions.

    • Final Verdict

  • **Recommendation:** **PROCEED (Top Tier Lowland Candidate)**
  • **Justification:** The Paraguay River terraces offer high carrying capacity and hydrological stability. It is an ideal site for a society focusing on large-scale agriculture and river-based trade.
