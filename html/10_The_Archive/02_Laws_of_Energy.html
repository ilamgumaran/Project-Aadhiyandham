The Laws of Energy: Efficiency Audit

1. The Energy Path

Every energy transformation has a cost. In Aadhiyandham, we minimize conversions to ensure maximum work-output from minimal resources.

The Energy Tax

Efficiency Audit Guide

  1. Map the Path: Draw the flow of energy from source to task.
  2. Feel the Heat: Identify components that get hot; heat is energy leaking from the system.
  3. Direct Work: Replace electric intermediate steps with direct mechanical work whenever possible.
  4. Maintain: Align shafts and lubricate with tallow/resin to reduce friction.
