Liebig's Law: System Diagnostic Guide

1. The Logic of Scarcity

A system is only as strong as its weakest link. By identifying the specific "Limiting Factor," you solve problems with surgical precision rather than wasting labor.

The Liebig Barrel

The level in the barrel can never rise above the shortest stave.

Diagnostic Step-by-Step

  1. Inventory: List every input required for the process (Water, Light, Minerals, etc.).
  2. Audit: Identify which input is the most scarce.
  3. The Pulse: Add a concentrated amount of the scarce input to a test section.
  4. Correct: If the test succeeds, focus 100% of labor on increasing that specific resource.
