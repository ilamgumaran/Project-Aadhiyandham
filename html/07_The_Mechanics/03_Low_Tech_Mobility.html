Low-Tech Mobility: Cargo Trike Construction

1. The Hybrid Chassis

By using **Structural Bamboo** for the frame and **Salvaged Steel** for high-stress joints, we create a vehicle that is lightweight, strong, and repairable.

Bamboo/Steel Frame Concept

How-To: The Composite Lug

  1. Miter: Carve bamboo ends to fit the steel parts.
  2. Wrap: Soak natural fiber (hemp/rope) in hot resin or hide glue.
  3. Bond: Wrap the fiber tightly around the joint in a cross-hatch pattern.
  4. Cure: Wait 48 hours for the resin to harden into a composite structure.
