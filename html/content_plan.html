Vocabulary and Definitions

Aadhiyandham
A Tamil portmanteau of Aadhi (Primordial Beginning) and Andham (Final End).
Bloomery
A type of furnace used for smelting iron from its oxides.
Crucible Steel
A high-carbon steel produced by co-fusing wrought iron and organic carbon.
Decadal
Relating to or covering a period of a decade (ten years).
E-Prime
A version of the English language that excludes all forms of the verb 'to be'.
Hydraulic Ram Pump
A cyclic water pump that uses the 'water hammer' effect.
Latrine
A toilet or outhouse, especially a communal one in a camp.
Line-Shaft
A power transmission system using a central rotating shaft.
Micro-climate
The climate of a very small or restricted area, especially when this differs from the climate of the surrounding area.
Recycling Cliff
The projected point in time when salvaged old-world materials will corrode.
Refugia
Geographical areas that remain life-supporting during periods of disruption.
Resilience
The capacity to withstand or to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness.
Sanitation
Conditions relating to public health, especially the provision of clean drinking water and adequate sewage disposal.
Terra Preta
A human-made, highly fertile soil type characterized by high concentrations of biochar.
Thermal Mass
A material's resistance to fluctuations in temperature, acting as a heat battery.
Wootz
A high-carbon steel produced by co-fusing wrought iron and organic carbon.
