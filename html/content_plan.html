Vocabulary and Definitions

Aadhiyandham
A Tamil portmanteau of Aadhi (Primordial Beginning) and Andham (Final End).
Biochar
A highly porous form of charcoal produced through pyrolysis.
Bloomery
A type of furnace used for smelting iron from its oxides.
Centered Null
The psychological baseline of Aadhiyandham.
Crucible Steel
A high-carbon steel produced by co-fusing wrought iron and organic carbon.
Decadal
Relating to or covering a period of a decade (ten years).
E-Prime
A version of the English language that excludes all forms of the verb 'to be'.
Entropy
The universal tendency toward disorder and the loss of useful energy.
Heirloom Seeds
Open-pollinated plant varieties passed down through generations.
Hydraulic Ram Pump
A cyclic water pump that uses the 'water hammer' effect.
Latrine
A toilet or outhouse, especially a communal one in a camp.
Line-Shaft

Recycling Cliff
