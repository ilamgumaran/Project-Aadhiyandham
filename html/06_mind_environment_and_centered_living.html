06 Mind, Environment & Centered Living

1. The Language of the Null (E-Prime)

To remove the ego and reduce conflict, we eliminate the verb "to be" from critical communication.

E-Prime Exercises

  • Instead of "This is mine," use "I hold this today."
  • Instead of "He is a bad leader," use "I disagree with his current actions."
  • Instead of "The sky is blue," use "The sky appears blue to me."

2. Sensory Architecture

Follow these restorative patterns to maintain mental clarity:

  • Light on Two Sides: Every room must have windows on two sides to eliminate glare and balance the nervous system.
  • The Alcove: Small, protected spaces within larger areas for quiet contemplation.
  • Compression: Minimalist design that focuses only on the essential.
