05 Resilience & Historical Wisdom

1. Learning from History

Survival requires flexibility. We contrast the **Ancestral Puebloans** (who survived by transforming their culture) with the **Greenland Norse** (who collapsed due to rigidity).

Models of Consensus Governance

  • Haudenosaunee: Bicameral consensus with matrilineal oversight.
  • Zapatista: "Lead by Obeying" - leaders as executors of community will.
  • Auroville: Selection through deep deliberation rather than competition.

2. The Recycling Cliff

Prepare for the exhaustion of salvaged materials. Primary smelting (Module 03) must be operational within 25 years before rust claims the legacy steel.
