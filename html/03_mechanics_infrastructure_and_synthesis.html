03 Mechanics, Infrastructure & Synthesis

1. Iron Smelting (The Bloomery)

Producing primary iron is essential to survive the "Recycling Cliff."

Bloomery Furnace Design

Clay/sand shaft capable of reaching 1200°C with charcoal.

How-To: The Bloomery Process

  1. Alternate layers of **hardwood charcoal** and **crushed iron ore**.
  2. Maintain a steady blast of air (bellows) for 5-8 hours.
  3. Extract the "Bloom" (iron/slag mass) from the base.
  4. "Shingle" the hot bloom on an anvil to squeeze out liquid slag.
