04 Knowledge & Biological Health

1. Foundational Knowledge

Understanding the human system is critical for living in harmony with the self.

The Brain's Rhythms

  • Serotonin: Triggered by morning light; regulates mood and focus.
  • Melatonin: Suppressed by blue light; regulates deep recovery during sleep.
  • Glymphatic Wash: Brain "cleaning" cycle active during deep sleep.

2. How the World Works: Simple Machines

Master these 6 patterns to achieve mechanical advantage:

  • Lever: MA = Distance to Input / Distance to Output.
  • Inclined Plane: MA = Length / Height.
  • Wheel & Axle: MA = Wheel Radius / Axle Radius.
  • Pulley: MA = Number of supporting rope segments.
  • Wedge: MA = Length / Width.
  • Screw: MA = Circumference / Pitch.
