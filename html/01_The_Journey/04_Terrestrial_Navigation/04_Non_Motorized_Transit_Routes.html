1. Primary National Trails / தேசிய பாதைகள்

  • U.S. Bicycle Route 21: Direct connection from Atlanta to Tennessee.
  • Appalachian Trail (AT): Southern terminus at Springer Mountain, GA.
  • TransAmerica Trail (USBR 76): The primary cross-country path to the Pacific Northwest.
  • Katy Trail (MO): Flat, safe rail-trail across Missouri.

2. Pack Animal Pacing

Follow the 50% Rule (15km per day) when traveling with mules or families to ensure survival health.
