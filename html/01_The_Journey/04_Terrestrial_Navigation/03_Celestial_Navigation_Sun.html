Celestial Navigation: Finding North with the Sun

வானியல் வழிசெலுத்தல்: சூரியனைக் கொண்டு வடக்கைக் கண்டறிதல்

1. The Shadow-Stick Method / நிழல்-குச்சி முறை

If tools are lost, the sun serves as your permanent guide.

Step-by-Step

  1. First Mark: Put a stick in the ground. Mark the shadow tip with a stone. (This is West).
  2. Wait: Wait 20 minutes for the shadow to move.
  3. Second Mark: Mark the new shadow tip. (This is East).
  4. Orientation: Stand with West on your left and East on your right. You are facing True North.

2. Handrails / கைப்பிடிகள்

Identify long features like rivers or ridges. Walking along these ensures you never get truly lost.

Finding North: The Shadow-Stick Method

The first mark is always West. The second mark is always East. The line pointing straight ahead from between your feet is North.
