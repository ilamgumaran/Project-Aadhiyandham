Orienting: Where Am I?

நோக்குநிலை: நான் எங்கே இருக்கிறேன்?

Before you can move toward a destination, you must understand your current relationship with the physical landscape. This is called Positioning.

Finding Your Current Location

  1. High-Ground Scan: Locate the highest hill or tree to identify major landmarks.
  2. Water-Flow Rule: Follow water upstream to move toward the mountain refugia.
  3. Triangulation: Use two known landmarks to pinpoint your intersection point.

Visual Triangulation (How to Find Yourself)

Point your eyes at two fixed landmarks. The spot where your "imaginary lines" cross is your exact location on the map.
