Why These Steps are Taken

1. Hub-Based Planning (ATL / SAV)

Importance: Efficiency and scale.

Rationale: Utilizing the world's most connected airport (ATL) and a premier Atlantic port (SAV) maximizes departure bandwidth before disruptions become permanent.

2. Multi-Modal Redundancy

Importance: Fail-safe transit.

Rationale: We provide land and sea alternatives for every region to ensure that if the sky closes, the road or sea remains viable.

3. Geographical Staging

Importance: Reducing exposure.

Rationale: Regional categorization allows for staged movements and the establishment of supply caches along transit corridors.

4. The Last Mile Strategy

Importance: Reaching the isolated refugia.

Rationale: Refugia are intentionally isolated. We cover the transition from international ports to high-elevation benches using local, resilient transport.
