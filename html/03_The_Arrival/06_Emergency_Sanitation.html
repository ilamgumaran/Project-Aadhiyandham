1. The Barrier Against Disease

According to WHO Standards, proper waste placement is the single most effective way to prevent outbreaks in a new settlement.

Siting Rules: The 30/50/6 Layout

Always place waste systems DOWNHILL and at least 30m away from water.

Technical Standards

  • The 30m Rule: Minimum distance from any spring or well.
  • The 1.5m Rule: The pit bottom must be 1.5m above the water table.
  • Sanitation: Use wood ash or charcoal dust daily to eliminate smells and flies.
