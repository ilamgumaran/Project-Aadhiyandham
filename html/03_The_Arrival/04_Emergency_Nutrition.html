Emergency Nutrition: The First 100 Days

A guide to the 10 universal emergency calorie sources found in mountain-protected environments.

The Scavenge List

  1. Pine Nuts: High fat/protein.
  2. Inner Bark (Cambium): Starch-rich (Pine/Birch).
  3. Cattail Roots: Pure starch.
  4. Dandelion Roots: Complex carbs.
  5. Pine Needle Tea: Vitamin C (Anti-scurvy).
  6. Wild Berries: Immediate glucose.
  7. Grass Seeds: Boiled gruel.
  8. Rock Tripe (Lichen): Emergency carbs (boil extensively).
  9. Earthworms/Grubs: High protein (must be cooked).
  10. Rose Hips: Massive Vitamin C.
