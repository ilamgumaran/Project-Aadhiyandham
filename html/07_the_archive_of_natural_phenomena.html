07 The Archive of Natural Phenomena

1. The Laws of Energy

Understanding the "currency" of the universe is essential for low-tech engineering.

Thermodynamics Summary

  • First Law (Conservation): "You can't get something for nothing." Energy cannot be created, only transformed.
  • Second Law (Entropy): "You can't even break even." Every conversion loses useful energy as heat. The universe tends toward disorder.

2. Liebig's Law of the Minimum

In agriculture, growth is limited by the scarcest resource (the shortest stave in the barrel), not the total amount available.
