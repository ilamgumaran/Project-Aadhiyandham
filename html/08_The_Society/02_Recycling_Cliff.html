The Recycling Cliff: Strategic Resource Management

1. Scavenging is Finite

Old-world steel will corrode. A civilization that cannot produce its own iron is doomed to regression.

Material Integrity Over Time

Annual Materials Audit

  1. Categorize: List tools as Stable, Degrading, or Failure.
  2. Calculate: Measure rust depth to estimate "Years to Failure."
  3. Action: Bloomery must be active 2 years before the first major tool failures.
