# Rationale and Importance: Group Dynamics

**Alignment:** Outcome 4: Psychological Centeredness

## 1. Why This Section Exists

Section 08 (The Mind) addresses individual psychological health. But post-collapse communities face intense group psychology pressures: power vacuums, scapegoating, in-group/out-group formation, and the natural emergence of informal hierarchies. This section will develop frameworks for maintaining healthy group dynamics at the community level, distinct from the interpersonal conflict resolution already covered in Section 08.

## 2. Planned Modules

*   **Power Vacuum Prevention** - Structural safeguards against charismatic-leader capture of consensus governance.
*   **Collective Grief and Trauma Processing** - Community-level rituals for processing shared loss (distinct from individual grief care).
*   **Social Cohesion Metrics** - Observable indicators that community trust is degrading, with early intervention protocols.
*   **Integration of Newcomers** - Protocols for absorbing refugees or travelers into existing community structures without destabilizing trust.

## 3. Status

This section is a structural placeholder. Modules will be developed after Section 08 (The Mind) and Section 10 (The Society) foundations are validated.
