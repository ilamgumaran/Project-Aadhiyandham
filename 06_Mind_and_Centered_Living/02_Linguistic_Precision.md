# Linguistic Precision: E-Prime Principles

Removing the verb "to be" to foster objective communication.

## The Null Communication
*   **Acknowledge Perspective:** Use "appears to me" or "I observe" instead of "is."
*   **Focus on Action:** Replace static labels with active verbs (e.g., "He works as a builder" vs. "He is a builder").
*   **Reduce Dogmatism:** Eliminating the false equation of an object with its label reduces emotional escalation and conflict.
*   **Transparency:** Forces the speaker to identify the actor, making communication accountable.
