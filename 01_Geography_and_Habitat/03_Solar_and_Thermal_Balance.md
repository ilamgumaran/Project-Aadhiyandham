# Solar and Thermal Balance

Biological and psychological health requires specific solar exposure.

## The Biological Dose
*   **Circadian Alignment:** 10–30 mins of direct outdoor morning sunlight (2,500 lux minimum).
*   **Vitamin D Synthesis:** 10–30 mins of midday UVB skin exposure (UV Index 3+) twice weekly.

## Site Aspect
*   **Northern Hemisphere:** Prioritize south-facing slopes.
*   **Southern Hemisphere:** Prioritize north-facing slopes.
*   *Rationale:* Maximizes winter solar gain for agriculture and passive heating.
