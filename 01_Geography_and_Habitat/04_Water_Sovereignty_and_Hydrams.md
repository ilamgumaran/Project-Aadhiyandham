# Water Sovereignty and Hydrams

Access to water must be independent of external energy.

## Gravity-Fed Systems
*   Priority: Sources higher than the settlement.
*   *Rationale:* Provides natural pressure for plumbing and irrigation.

## Zero-Energy Movement (The Hydraulic Ram Pump)
If water is below the settlement, use a **Hydram**.
*   **The Principle:** Uses the "water hammer" effect to pump water uphill.
*   **Performance:** Can lift water 10–20 times the height of the supply head.
*   **Zero-Maintenance:** No electricity or fuel required; 24/7 operation.
