# Topographical Resilience

A location must provide safety from sea-level rise and extreme storms.

## Criteria
*   **Elevation:** Minimum 100m above current sea level.
*   **Drainage:** Land must naturally shed excess water.
*   **Wind Buffering:** Proximity to high ridges or mountain ranges to block major storm events.
*   **Thermal Belts:** Identifying valleys where warm air is trapped, extending growing seasons and reducing heating needs.
